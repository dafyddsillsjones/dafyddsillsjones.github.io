Screen practice · Documentary · Virtual Production · AI

Work made between place, memory, language and the moving image.

I am a Welsh artist-researcher, filmmaker and academic based in Aotearoa New Zealand. My work explores documentary form, virtual production, AI media, minority-language culture, and the traces that remain when real places are translated into digital systems.

View selected work Get in touch

field / archive / scan / signal

My practice moves between the documentary image and its technological afterlives: landscape as evidence, performance as transmission, language as memory, and virtual production as a way of asking what remains accountable to the place from which it emerges.
