Documentary · Virtual Production · AI · Place

Work made between landscape, language and the moving image.

I am a Welsh artist-researcher, filmmaker and academic based in Aotearoa New Zealand. My work explores documentary form, virtual production, AI media, minority-language culture, and the traces that remain when real places are translated into digital systems.

Selected films Contact

My practice asks how documentary images, virtual environments and networked performances can remain accountable to the places, languages and communities from which they emerge.

Current projects

Research as situated practice

Portals/Traces: Material Ecologies of Virtual Production

A research programme developing international workflows for shared virtual production, live mocap, AI image systems, real-time translation, and cross-site creative exchange.

Extending Virtual Production

A creative research initiative exploring virtual production beyond studio spectacle, toward pedagogy, documentary practice, expanded screen cultures and public-facing experimentation.

AI, Documentary and the Trace

Research and editorial work examining how AI systems alter questions of evidence, authorship, indexicality, memory and documentary ethics.
